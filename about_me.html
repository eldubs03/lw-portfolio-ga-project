Lauren Wilson is a graphic and web designer at Redpoint, a New York City based boutique-marketing agency. At Redpoint, we design for digital - web sites, digital campaigns, and e-newsletters and are expanding everyday into print with ads, brochures and trade-show collateral. Through my commercial work in design, I help influence consumer behavior both in broad strokes and drilling down deep into a particular market segment. Along with Redpoint’s account team, I create PR and marketing campaigns that are matched precisely to the "brand voice" required, ensuring success whether the audience reflects indulgent luxury or functional mass market.
